# Privacy Policy — Weatherlane

**Last updated: May 3, 2026**

Weatherlane ("the app") is built and maintained by Kacy James. This policy describes what information the app accesses, how it is used, and what is shared with third parties.

---

## Information the app accesses

### Location
Weatherlane can access your device's location to show weather for where you are right now. This is optional — you can use the app with only manually added cities and never grant location permission. When location access is granted:

- Your coordinates are sent directly to **Apple WeatherKit** to retrieve a forecast. They are not sent to any server operated by Weatherlane.
- Your location is never logged, stored remotely, or shared with any advertising or analytics service.

### Saved cities
City names you add to your list are stored locally on your device using iOS system storage. They are not uploaded anywhere.

### Temperature preference
Your chosen temperature unit (°F or °C) is stored locally on your device. It is never transmitted.

---

## Third-party services

### Apple WeatherKit
Weather data is provided by Apple's WeatherKit service. Your location coordinates are transmitted to Apple in order to fetch forecasts. Apple's privacy policy applies: [https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy)

### Unsplash
Background photos are fetched from the Unsplash API using a city name as the search query. No personally identifiable information is included in these requests. Unsplash's privacy policy applies: [https://unsplash.com/privacy](https://unsplash.com/privacy)

---

## What the app does not do

- Weatherlane does not require an account or collect any personal information such as name, email address, or phone number.
- Weatherlane does not display advertising.
- Weatherlane does not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties beyond the service calls described above.
- Weatherlane does not use analytics SDKs or tracking libraries.

---

## Data retention

No user data is retained on Weatherlane's servers because no user data is sent to Weatherlane's servers. All app data (saved cities, preferences) lives on your device and is removed when you delete the app.

---

## Children's privacy

Weatherlane does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app contains no features that solicit personal information.
