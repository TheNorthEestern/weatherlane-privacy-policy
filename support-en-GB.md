# Weatherlane Support

For help, questions, or feedback, email us at **support@studentdriver.co**. We aim to reply within 1 to 2 business days.

When you reach out, it helps to include:

- Your iPhone model and iOS version
- The Weatherlane version
- A short description of what happened

## Common questions

**How do I manage my Weatherlane Pro subscription?**
Open the Settings app, tap your name, then Subscriptions, then Weatherlane Pro. You can change plans or cancel from there.

**My widget isn't updating.**
Open the app once so it can refresh the cached forecast. If it's still stale, long-press the widget, choose Edit Widget, and confirm the right location is selected.

**Why does the temperature look wrong?**
Open the app menu and check whether you're set to Celsius or Fahrenheit. We default to your region's convention, but the toggle is there if you want to switch.
